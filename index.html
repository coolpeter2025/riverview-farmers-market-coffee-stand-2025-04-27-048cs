Our Story

Founded in 2020, Delightful Bean began as a passion project between two friends who shared a vision of bringing premium coffee culture to the Riverview Farmers Market. What started as a small cart has grown into a beloved community fixture, serving hundreds of satisfied customers daily.

Our mission is to create exceptional coffee experiences while fostering community connections and environmental sustainability. We believe in the power of quality coffee to bring people together and make every day a little more special. Our core values of authenticity, sustainability, and community guide everything we do.

Common Questions

What are your operating hours?

We're open Tuesday through Sunday, 7am to 2pm, following the Farmers Market schedule. Closed on Mondays.

Do you offer non-dairy alternatives?

Yes! We provide a variety of plant-based options including oat, almond, and soy milk at no extra charge.

How does your reusable cup program work?

Bring your own clean cup and receive a $0.50 discount on any drink. We also sell branded reusable cups that come with your first drink free.

Where do you source your beans?

We partner with local roasters who source directly from small-scale farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.

Do you offer wholesale or catering?

Yes, we provide catering services for events and wholesale options for local businesses. Contact us for custom packages.
